Originally from Scotland but now based in the South East, Chantelle performs in a wide variety of musical genres including jazz, funk, soul, pop – i.e. most contemporary styles. After studying jazz & commercial music in Newcastle, Chantelle went on to perform with her band in Japan, the Middle East and New York City.  Her credits include radio and television work as well as writing and recording both for herself and for other artists.  She is the lead singer in original band Berakah, and is currently recording her first album.
